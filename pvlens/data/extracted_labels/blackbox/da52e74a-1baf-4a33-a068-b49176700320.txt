WARNING: CARDIOVASCULAR RISK ASSOCIATED WITH SMOKING<br>Cigarette smoking increases the risk of serious cardiovascular events from combination oral contraceptive use. This risk increases with age, particularly in women over 35 years of age, and with the number of cigarettes smoked.
