For patients with short-term insomnia, instructions in the prescription should indicate that Restoril ™ (temazepam) should be used for short periods of time (7 to 10 days). The clinical trials performed in support of efficacy were 2 weeks in duration with the final formal assessment of sleep latency performed at the end of treatment.
