The following adverse reactions associated with the use of ephedrine sulfate were identified in the literature. Because these reactions are reported voluntarily from a population of uncertain size, it is not always possible to estimate their frequency reliably or to establish a causal relationship to drug exposure.<br>Gastrointestinal disorders: Nausea, vomiting<br>Cardiac disorders: Tachycardia, palpitations (thumping heart), reactive hypertension, bradycardia, ventricular ectopics, R-R variability<br>Nervous system disorders: Dizziness<br>Psychiatric disorders: Restlessness<br>Most common adverse reactions during treatment: nausea, vomiting, and tachycardia.
